All Features

🚀 Everything your gym needs

IronBook packs every tool a modern gym requires — no paperwork, no spreadsheets, no confusion.

👤 Member Management

Add, edit, search members. Track membership expiry, auto-renewals, and member profiles with photos, contact details, and membership history.

📋 Plans

Create membership plans with custom pricing, duration, and features. Assign plans to members with one tap. Monthly, quarterly, yearly — you decide.

💰 Payments

Record payments with discounts, multiple methods (Cash/UPI/Card). Payment history, digital receipts, dues tracking and automated reminders.

📷 Attendance

Check-in/check-out tracking with QR code scanner. Face recognition attendance, daily logs and attendance history for every member.

👥 Staff Management

Role-based staff access (owner 👑, admin 💼, trainer 🏾‍♂️, staff 🔧). Staff attendance, payroll, and performance tracking.

📈 Expenses

Track gym expenses by category. Monthly expense reports, comparison with revenue, and cost analysis to maximize your profits.

📊 Reports

Export PDF/CSV/Excel reports for members, revenue, attendance and expenses. Share, print or email directly from the app.

🔔 Notifications

Real-time alerts via Supabase Realtime. In-app notifications for due payments 📣, new members 🎉, expiry reminders ⏰ and system updates.

📥 Import/Export

Bulk import members from CSV files. Export all reports in multiple formats. Move data in and out without manual entry.

🌎 Multi-language

English 🇬🇧, Hindi 🇮🇳, Marathi 🇲🇴 — full UI translated for your local gym staff and members.

🔒 Security

Row-Level Security (RLS) on all tables, field whitelisting, role escalation protection, CSV formula injection protection and more 🛡️.

⚡ AI-Powered

Predictive insights for revenue, churn, and member engagement. Smart reminders, automated billing and AI-driven recommendations.

Tech Stack

🛠️ Built with modern tech

IronBook is powered by a battle-tested stack — Flutter for the frontend, Supabase for the backend.

🚀 Flutter

Cross-platform UI framework. Same codebase for Android, Web, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux.

⚡ Supabase

Open-source Firebase alternative. PostgreSQL, Auth, Realtime, Storage — all in one.

📈 Riverpod

State management with StateNotifier + FutureProvider. Predictable, testable, scalable.

🔗 GoRouter

Declarative routing with auth guards and shell routes. Secure page transitions.

📄 Reports Engine

PDF, Excel, CSV export. Print-ready reports for members, revenue, attendance and expenses.

📷 Mobile Scanner

QR code scanning for member check-in/check-out. Fast, reliable, contactless.
