# Brilliants — Investor & Client Q&A

---

## Investor Q&A

**Q: What does Brilliants do in one line?**
AI-powered business automation platforms for Indian MSMEs — starting with gyms (IronBook), expanding into HRMS, billing, and manufacturing.

**Q: What problem are you solving?**
Indian MSMEs (65M+ businesses) still run on manual processes, disconnected systems, and expensive legacy software that wasn't built for them. We give them enterprise-grade AI at 1/10th the cost — mobile-first, offline-capable, GST-ready.

**Q: TAM / market size?**
India has 65M+ MSMEs contributing 30% to GDP. The gym software market alone is Rs 500Cr+. Our Vision 2030 targets Rs 100Cr+ ARR by Year 3 across 5+ verticals.

**Q: What's your traction?**
50+ gyms live, Rs 2Cr+ monthly collections processed, 200+ active users, 4.9★ rating, 98% satisfaction. Launched 2025, IronBook live with paying customers.

**Q: Revenue model?**
SaaS — Freemium (Starter Free for 25 members) → Pro at ₹499/mo → Enterprise at ₹1,499/mo. Add-on products at ₹299-399/mo. Annual plans at ~16-17% discount.

**Q: What's your moat?**
1. Vertical-specific AI — shared intelligence engine that improves across all products
2. First-mover in underserved MSME verticals
3. India-first architecture (offline, vernacular, UPI, GST)
4. Fast deployment — days vs months for legacy ERP

**Q: Who are your competitors?**
OneFitPlus (₹1,500-5,000/mo), WodGuru ($29-79/mo), PushPress — all more expensive, none with AI. On the MSME side: Marg ERP, Vyapar, Busy — desktop-based, no AI, not mobile-first.

**Q: Tech stack?**
Flutter (cross-platform), Spring Boot backend, Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth), AI engine with Computer Vision, NLP, Predictive ML. Hosted on cloud, IoT gateway for manufacturing.

**Q: What's the unit economics?**
Freemium drives adoption (zero CAC for organic growth). Pro converts at ₹5,988/yr. Add-ons stack to ₹9,576/yr per customer. Enterprise at ₹17,988/yr. No paid marketing yet — all organic/WhatsApp/referral.

**Q: Are you funded?**
Bootstrapped / pre-seed. Open to speaking with angel investors and VCs who understand the MSME SaaS opportunity in India.

**Q: Founding team?**
Vikas Kamble, Founder & CEO. Mechanical engineer, 5+ years across automotive and pharma manufacturing. He lived the problem of manual processes in Indian manufacturing.

**Q: How will you acquire customers at scale?**
1. WhatsApp-driven demo-to-conversion funnel
2. Hindi/regional content marketing
3. Gym franchise networks
4. Partner/distributor model in tier-2/3 cities

## Client/Customer Q&A

**Q: Your software is new. Why should I trust you?**
We have 50+ gyms already live processing Rs 2Cr+ in monthly collections. Avg rating 4.9★. You can start free for up to 25 members — no risk. Plus 30-day money-back guarantee.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
Yes — cloud-hosted on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security. Each business gets isolated data. We use HTTPS end-to-end. We don't share or sell your data.

**Q: Do I need internet all the time?**
No. The Android app works offline — attendance, billing, member data sync when you're back online. Built specifically for Indian internet conditions.

**Q: Can I try before paying?**
Absolutely. Starter plan is free for up to 25 members with full features. No credit card required. Upgrade only when you outgrow it.

**Q: What if I don't like it?**
30-day money-back guarantee on paid plans. No questions asked. You keep your data export.

**Q: Will this work for my specific business type?**
IronBook is built specifically for gyms & fitness studios. We also have Smart HRMS (any business), Smart Billing (retail/QSR), and Smart Factory (manufacturing). Pick what fits you.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**
Yes — full-featured Android app + web dashboard. Members can also check in, view plans, and get notifications from their phones.

**Q: What support do you offer?**
WhatsApp support (+91-9146121280), email (contact@brilliants.in), and priority support on Pro/Enterprise plans. Response within 24 hours.

**Q: Do you handle GST billing?**
Yes — GST-ready invoicing built in. Applicable for IronBook billing and Smart Billing products.

**Q: Can I migrate from my current software?**
Yes — we have import tools for member data, plans, and billing history. Contact us and we'll help with migration.

**Q: What languages does the software support?**
Currently English and Hindi. Marathi coming soon. Built with multi-lingual support from day one.
