7 Questions Every MSME Owner Asks Before Buying Software — Answered

11 July 2026 · 6 min read · By Brilliants Team

If you run a small or medium business in India, you've probably hesitated before buying software. Will it actually help? Is it secure? Can I afford it? What if it doesn't work for my type of business?

These are fair questions. Most software in India is built for enterprises with big budgets, IT teams, and dedicated servers. MSMEs have different needs — and different concerns.

Here are the 7 most common questions we hear from MSME owners, answered honestly.

1. "Is my data safe?"

This is the #1 concern — and rightly so. Your member data, billing records, employee information — it's the backbone of your business.

Modern SaaS platforms like Brilliants use bank-grade encryption (HTTPS end-to-end) and Row-Level Security in the database. What does that mean? Your data is isolated — you see only your business data, and nobody else can access it, not even other customers on the same platform.

Behind the scenes, we use Supabase (built on PostgreSQL) — the same infrastructure trusted by millions of applications worldwide. We don't share or sell your data.

Bottom line: Cloud software today is often more secure than keeping files on your office computer or a local server that nobody has patched in years.

2. "Can I try before I pay?"

Yes — and you should. Any software that asks for a commitment before you've used it is a red flag.

With Brilliants, you can start free for up to 25 members. No credit card required. No time limit on the free tier. You get the full feature set — face recognition attendance, automated billing, reports, everything.

Only upgrade when you outgrow the free tier or need more capacity. And if you decide it's not for you, you keep your data export.

3. "Will it work for my specific business?"

This depends on what you do. Brilliants builds vertical-specific software — not one-size-fits-all ERPs:

  • Gyms & fitness studios → IronBook (face attendance, billing, member management)
  • Retail & QSR → Smart Billing (GST invoicing, inventory, payments)
  • Manufacturing → Smart Factory (IoT monitoring, predictive maintenance)
  • Any business → Smart HRMS (payroll, attendance, leave management)

Each product is built specifically for its industry — not a generic CRM with a renamed module.

4. "Do I need internet all the time?"

This is India. Internet is not always reliable. We get it.

That's why our Android app works fully offline — you can mark attendance, log payments, add members, all without internet. When you're back online, it syncs automatically.

Most international software fails here. They assume always-on connectivity. We don't.

No setup fees. No hidden charges. Cancel anytime.

6. "What if I don't like it after paying?"

Every paid plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you don't see improvement in your operations, we refund 100% — no questions asked.

We're confident because most of our users see results in the first week itself. Billing time drops from hours to minutes. Attendance becomes automatic. Member renewals go up because reminders are automated.

7. "Can I use it on my phone?"

For most MSME owners, the phone is their primary device. That's why every Brilliants product is mobile-first.

The Android app gives you full access — member management, billing, reports, attendance, staff management. The web dashboard is there when you need a bigger screen, but your phone is where the business runs.

The real question: "Can I afford to keep running on manual processes?" Spreadsheets, paper registers, WhatsApp billing — these cost you hours every day. Software doesn't just save money; it saves time. And for a business owner, time is the scarcest resource.
