Best Gym Management Software in India 2026 — Complete Guide

8 July 2026 · 8 min read · By Brilliants Team

If you own a gym in India, you already know the pain. Members not paying on time, attendance fudging, staff tracking on paper, expenses scattered across notebooks. You're running a fitness business but spending half your time on admin.

Gym management software fixes this. But with so many options — IronBook, OneFitPlus, WodGuru, PushPress, and others — how do you choose the right one?

In this guide, we compare the top gym management software available in India in 2026. We look at features, pricing, pros, cons, and who each is best for.

Why Your Gym Needs Management Software

Before we dive into comparisons, let's look at what you gain by switching from manual operations:

  • Automated billing — No more chasing members for monthly fees. Auto-reminders, auto-charges, digital receipts.
  • Real-time attendance — Face recognition or QR check-in. Know exactly who's in the gym and when.
  • Member management — Profiles, plans, expiry dates, renewal alerts — all in one place.
  • Staff management — Track trainer hours, commissions, schedules.
  • Reports & insights — Daily profit, revenue trends, member churn, class performance.
  • Multi-location — Manage multiple gyms from a single dashboard.

Did you know? Gyms using management software report 87% faster billing, 18% higher renewal rates, and save an average of 10+ hours per week on admin. (Brilliants internal data, 2026)

2. OneFitPlus

Best for: Premium gym chains with large budgets.

OneFitPlus is an enterprise-grade platform popular among larger chains. It offers robust member management and billing but comes with higher pricing and is often overkill for smaller gyms.

Pricing: ₹1,500 - ₹5,000/month depending on features. Annual contracts required.

3. WodGuru

Best for: CrossFit boxes and functional fitness studios.

WodGuru focuses on workout programming and class scheduling. While excellent for the programming side, it lacks Indian billing features (GST invoicing, UPI payments) and comprehensive business reporting.

Pricing: $29 - $79/month (~₹2,400 - ₹6,600)

4. PushPress

Best for: US/European gyms with international payment processing.

PushPress is a solid platform but not optimized for India. No GST invoices, limited UPI/cash payment support, and US-based support in US time zones.

How to Choose the Right Gym Software

Ask yourself these questions before deciding:

  1. How many members do you have? Under 100? Most software will work. Over 500? Look for scalability.
  2. Do you need AI features? Face recognition, churn prediction, auto reports — IronBook leads here.
  3. What's your budget? Under ₹1,000/month? IronBook Starter is your best bet.
  4. Do you need Indian billing? GST invoices, UPI payments, cash handling — most international software doesn't support these.
  5. Single gym or chain? Multi-location support is available in IronBook Pro and OneFitPlus.
