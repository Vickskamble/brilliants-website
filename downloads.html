Installation Guide

How to install the APK

Android blocks installs from outside the Play Store by default — here's how to allow it, safely, just for this one file.

1

Download the APK

Tap "Download APK" above. Your phone may show a browser warning — tap "Download anyway".

2

Allow this source

Open the downloaded file. If prompted, allow your browser to "Install unknown apps" — this permission only applies to that app.

3

Install

Tap "Install" and wait a few seconds. Android may show a Play Protect scan — this is normal for direct downloads.

4

Open & sign in

Launch the app and sign in with the credentials shared by your Brilliants onboarding contact.

Only download Brilliants apps from brilliants.in or a link shared directly by our team. We'll never ask you to install an APK from any other source.
